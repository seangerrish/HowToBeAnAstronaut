What does it really mean to be a student at one of the world’s best public universities? It means choosing from one of more than 250 areas of study. Taking advantage of our amazing opportunities. And, regardless of your major, being guided by caring faculty mentors who include Pulitzer Prize and Guggenheim winners, National Academy of Science members, MacArthur Fellows, and even a few Emmy winners. So, go ahead—think big. Because, you’re at a place where you can make those dreams come true.
