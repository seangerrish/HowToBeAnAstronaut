UCLA Undergraduate Majors

School of Theater, Film, and Television

 

Film and Television (B.A.)*

Theater (B.A.)

Individual Field of Concentration (B.A.)

* - The Film and Television major is open to junior level transfer applicants only.

 

For more information on a specific major, please refer to Choosing an Area of Study, or to the UCLA General Catalog.

Applications to the the School of the Theater, Film and Television are limited to the fall quarter and are reviewed for admission by the department to which an applicant applies. For Theater majors, this review may include an audition. Transfer applicants must complete lower division preparation course work specific to their intended majors.

Note: Recent changes to the Department of Theater curriculum now make it impossible for Theater majors to change their major to Film and Television at the end of the sophomore year.
